About us

A must for éclair lovers! Our bakery offers an exquisite selection of handcrafted éclairs with rich, creamy fillings and delicate toppings. Each éclair is made fresh daily using high-quality ingredients. Enjoy our limited-time Spring Specials, featuring flavors inspired by fresh seasonal ingredients like Lemon Blossom, Strawberry Delight, and Lavender Honey. At Peaceful Dessert, we believe in the power of love and natural ingredients. Every baked good is made to order, ensuring the freshest and most delicious experience for you. The result? Pure indulgence that will leave you craving more!

Our Story

Peaceful Dessert is more than just a bakery; it's a dream brought to life by four passionate individuals from different corners of the world. Meet the team: Dona from Sri Lanka, Valeriia from Ukraine, and Rain from Bangladesh. Together, we bring a unique blend of cultures, traditions, and flavors to every creation. In 2023, we started our journey in the heart of Kamloops, Canada, with a shared vision: to create desserts that not only taste amazing but also tell a story. Our diverse backgrounds inspire us to experiment with flavors, techniques, and ideas, resulting in a harmonious balance of tradition and innovation. From the bustling streets of Colombo to the snowy landscapes of Ukraine, and the vibrant markets of Bangladesh, our experiences have shaped our love for baking. At Peaceful Dessert, we celebrate diversity, creativity, and the joy of sharing delicious treats with our community. Every bite of our desserts carries the essence of our journey, and we hope it brings a little piece of the world to your table.
